Peaking EQ filter that boosts or cuts frequencies around the center frequency.

## Parameters

- **frequency**: Center frequency in Hz (default: 350)
- **Q**: Bandwidth/quality factor (default: 1)
- **gain**: Boost/cut in dB (default: 0)
