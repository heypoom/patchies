The `screen` object captures your screen, window,
or browser tab as a live video stream.

## Getting Started

Create a `screen` object and click the monitor icon or
send a `bang` to start capture. Your browser will prompt
you to select a screen, window, or tab to share.

The captured video is output as a texture that can be
chained to visual objects like `glsl`, `hydra`, or `p5`.

## Stopping Capture

Click the red stop button on the node, or end the screen
share from your browser's sharing controls.
