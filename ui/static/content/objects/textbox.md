## Lock Mode

Click the lock button (top-right) to lock the textbox. When locked:

- Resize handles are hidden
- The "textbox" drag label is hidden
- Inlet and outlet handles are hidden (unless easy connect is enabled)
- The node cannot be dragged

Click the lock button again to unlock.
